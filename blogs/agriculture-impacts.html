Project Overview

Research Questions

  • Do these effects occur immediately or with a lag?
  • What is the role of urbanisation and population growth in these dynamics?

Insights & Implications

The delayed effect of agriculture on forests means that today's land decisions will affect ecosystems for years ‚Äì highlighting the need for forward‚Äëlooking sustainable land management. Urbanisation‚Äôs dual role (environmental pressure vs. health gains) calls for integrated urban‚Äëenvironmental planning. The strong malnutrition‚Äìagriculture correlation suggests that expanding agriculture can improve food security, but must be paired with health and nutrition programmes to translate into better child health.
